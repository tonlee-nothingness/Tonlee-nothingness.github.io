By Dhruv Sharma | Feb 4, 2026

Abstract

This paper proposes that Nature operates as a "causality killing machine" within an overall Null-Universe. It presents a no-boundary, no-unique-beginning proposal for the universal phenomenon. Fundamental concepts such as time and mass are argued to be non-absolute, arising solely from our limited scope and dissolving to nullity at infinite or zero scope.

Core Arguments

  • Dark Matter & Energy: These are not particles or fields. Instead, Dark Matter is an effective weight portraying the lineage towards an intrinsic pathway to minimize causality. Dark Energy is the extrinsic pathway to the same goal.
  • [cite_start]Black Holes: Described as "saints among us mortals". They always expand and evaporate external space-time via Hawking radiation, which effectively kills causality in the surrounding space-time.
  • The Mismatch Model: Matter evolves through a natural progress towards nullity. [cite_start]A material is never complete; it transforms until it reduces to gravitational waves and photons by meeting its exact counterpart.
